What is Neurodevelopmental Screening?

Developmental screening helps assess your child's growth by using short tests or questionnaires. These tools, based on research, ask about your child's language, movement, thinking, behavior, and emotions. Your child should be screened if you or your doctor have a concern.However, developmental screening is a regular part of some of the well-child visits for all children even if there is not a known concern.
